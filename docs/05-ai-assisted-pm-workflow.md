# AI-Assisted PM Workflow

## Objective

Use AI tools to accelerate product discovery, technical analysis, and artifact generation while preserving decision quality.

## Workflow

1. Problem framing
- Generate structured hypothesis trees from ambiguous stakeholder asks.
- Produce alternative scope definitions (MVP, target, stretch).

2. Technical research acceleration
- Summarize architecture options and known implementation patterns.
- Build risk and dependency checklists before engineering commitment.

3. Prototype and specification drafting
- Produce draft PRDs, API contract outlines, and rollout plans.
- Convert requirements into acceptance criteria and test scenarios.

4. Communication and alignment
- Create role-specific versions of the same decision context for exec, engineering, and operations.
- Maintain decision logs with rationale and rejected alternatives.

## Guardrails

- Never delegate final product decisions blindly to AI outputs.
- Validate assumptions against system constraints and business goals.
- Keep human accountability for prioritization and risk acceptance.

## Outcome

AI shortens research and drafting cycles so more time is invested in strategic tradeoff decisions and execution quality.
