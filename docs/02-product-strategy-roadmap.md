# Strategy and Roadmap (12-Month Model)

## Strategic Objective

Build and evolve platform foundations that let product delivery teams ship faster with lower reliability and security risk.

## North-Star Outcomes

- Improve system trust: reduce high-severity incidents and customer-facing failures
- Improve delivery velocity: reduce cycle time for feature release and rollback risk
- Improve efficiency: increase workload handled per cloud dollar

## Roadmap by Quarter

### Q1: Foundation and Baselines

- Define critical service boundaries and dependency map
- Establish reliability baselines and incident taxonomy
- Align product and engineering on platform KPIs and ownership

### Q2: Reliability Hardening

- Prioritize top reliability bottlenecks and failure modes
- Roll out idempotency and consistency controls on key write paths
- Introduce operational runbooks for incident response and escalation

### Q3: Security and Compliance Maturity

- Strengthen access control enforcement and privileged operation handling
- Formalize risk review checkpoints in roadmap intake
- Instrument auditability for sensitive actions

### Q4: Scale and Cost Optimization

- Optimize high-volume paths for performance and cloud spend
- Improve observability to support capacity planning
- Rationalize low-ROI infrastructure workloads and reduce waste

## Prioritization Framework

- Customer trust impact (reliability/security)
- Engineering leverage (reuse and productivity lift)
- Financial impact (cost reduction and margin protection)
- Execution risk (complexity, dependencies, and rollout safety)

## Expected Deliverables

- Outcome-driven roadmap with explicit success metrics
- Quarterly platform health reviews
- Decision logs for key architectural/product tradeoffs
